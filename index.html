What is the most significant thing you have learned about object oriented?
I have learned to look at websites in terms of individual objects. From a design stand point it is much easier to start in the object sense and work on one section at a time. Something big and complicated is much easier to design when broken into chunks
What is the most significant thing you have learned about the effect of the frontend on the backend?
I have learned that consistency is king! Especially when naming class objects and state variables.
Why is encapsulation so important?
It's important to hide values and state of structured data object inside a class which prevents unauthorized access.
